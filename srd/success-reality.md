### Regulatory Decision

**AltruPets is a coordination platform, NOT a financial intermediary.**

- Donations flow peer-to-peer: donor -> org bank account (via SINPE/bank transfer)
- Vet subsidy payments flow: municipality -> vet clinic (facilitated by AltruPets)
- AltruPets NEVER holds, processes, or routes funds through its accounts
- This avoids SUGEF oversight (Ley 7786, Arts. 15/15 bis) and associated compliance costs

## Key Assumptions

1. Costa Rica has ~82 municipalities (cantones). Capturing 7 = ~8.5% market share.
2. Average vet clinic processes 6 subsidized cases/month on the platform.
3. Rescue organizations use the platform for FREE. They are value creators, not revenue sources.
4. Donors contribute via peer-to-peer transfers (SINPE), not through AltruPets.
5. In-kind donations (food, medical supplies, toys) are first-class alongside monetary donations.
